Smart Monitor: A Magic Mirror Dashboard

Smart Monitor is a web-based dashboard designed to be run on a Magic Mirror setup or external monitor in order to allow you to see the most important information for your productivity at a glance. It displays your current time, date, and upcoming tasks, as well as your calendar events from Google Calendar and upcoming Hack Club events. In addition, it allows you to design a fully customizable time-blocking schedule for your current day and the following day.
